I am a graduate student in the Biology PhD program at Stanford University, advised by Benjamin Good and Dmitri Petrov. My research interests include evolutionary dynamics, population genetics theory, and experimental evolution.


Prior to starting graduate school, I was a research assistant with Fyodor Kondrashov, where I worked on projects in molecular evolution. I hold a BSc in Physics from Saint Petersburg Polytechnic University.
